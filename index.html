Celebrating women's past + empowering our future

Spring Forward is a month-long celebration of the role of women in digital culture and runs throughout March in parallel with Women's History Month.

Our mission

To encourage women to take a greater role in digital by creating a platform that promotes digital community events organised by women for women.

CodeBar

Tuesday 3rd March 68 Middle St Brighton

CodeBar runs free weekly workshops for women, LGBTQ and people who are underrepresented in the tech industry to get to grips with the basics of programming and web development.

Find out more >

Curiosity Hub

Saturday 14th March 68 Middle St Brighton

Celebrate Mother's Day with creative tech workshops! Girls aged 11-16, along with their mums (or other adult female mentor), are invited create a short stop-motion movie - just bring your imaginations!

Find out more >

SheCodesBrighton

Monday 16th March 68 Middle St Brighton

SheCodes is a community of female software developers with a goal to reach 50% female software developers in the hi-tech industry within a decade. Join us for "Tech in Ten", a special event that will showcase a series of 10 minutes tech talks by women.

Find out more >

300 Seconds

Wednesday 18th March 68 Middle St Brighton

300 Seconds is a series of events by and for the digital community. Their unique format supports women in gaining the confidence and experience in public speaking while our audiences (men and women!) enjoy diverse talks in a fun and engaging environment.

Find out more >

Pixels & Prosecco

Friday 20th March 68 Middle St Brighton

An evening featuring 5 women in the games industry speaking about the great things they do, with the added bonus of plenty of wine and cake.

Find out more >

SheSays Brighton

Tuesday 24th March Hotel du Vin Brighton

SheSays "Speak up!" We've invited four amazing MDs of Brighton digital companies to take part in a panel. They'll discuss topics including integrity, diversity, growth and community. We're expecting a lively discussion, with you the audience welcome to join in with gusto!

Find out more >

Ladies That UX

Thursday 26th March 68 Middle St Brighton

Ladies That UX is a friendly, welcoming and collaborative community run by women interested in user experience. The Spring Forward event is going to be a special one as we have talks for the first time. Our amazing speakers will be sharing tips on confidence, perfectionism, leadership and of course, UX!

Find out more >
